About Outlier

Outlier Construction LLC was founded in Medford, Oregon in 2017 and has quickly become one of Southern Oregon’s largest commercial contractors. Located in the heart of the Rogue Valley, our staff of over 45 employees completes more than 30 projects annually across the Southern region of the State of Oregon.

The team

We are proven performers in Oregon public and private contracting with active CM/GC, Design-Build, and Hard Bid projects always in flight. With over 250 years of collective experience, our team stands ready to successfully complete your commercial construction project.
