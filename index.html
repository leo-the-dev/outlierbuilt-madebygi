Our work

A Southern Oregon based real estate development and construction firm.

The Outlier development team combines a network of real estate professionals with financial know-how to analyze, budget, and construct residential and commercial projects. In addition, Outlier has relationships in the banking industry as well as with top real estate agents and commercial brokers. Outlier’s construction team has experience in multifamily housing, custom single-family, large commercial developments, and medical construction.

Outlier & you

Our collaborative approach, combined with our team of experts, will ensure that your project runs smoothly without concern. The decision to create Outlier was based on a strong belief in the value of teamwork and respect for others engaged in the process.

Collaboration

The Outlier approach to orchestrating a construction project is to engage a team that includes the client, subcontractors, employees, and design teams to work in a collaborative partnership rather than individual entities. We pride ourselves in the ability to quickly and effectively find solutions to any challenges that may arise while ensuring a pleasant building experience for our clients.

Safety

At Outlier, we pride ourselves on our commitment to safety. We have implemented programs including a safety committee, and monthly safety meetings. These are lead by our Safety Director and attended by all safety committee members as well as all new hires. In addition, new hires are required to attend safety meetings for the first year of employment. Outlier also has weekly job site safety meetings to address current topics for onsite safety.

Value Engineering

During the design process, the Outlier team will employ the practice of “Value Engineering” as a means of optimizing a project’s value. Working with local sub-contractors, Outlier will identify products and systems that will save the owner money while preserving the intended function and quality. These cost-saving measures can thus be implemented without affecting appearance, function, or useful life.

Project Management

Our expert project managers will be the main point of contact. For instance, this includes overseeing the budget and value engineering as required, helping with subcontractor recruitment, and assisting in making bid packages for subcontractors to bid out. Once the job has started, they will be the one overseeing the day-to-day activities, ensuring a smooth and efficient project.

Commitment to Quality

Outlier has earned a reputation for innovation and reliable performance that we believe sets us apart from the competition. We really do see ourselves as “the outlier” in this industry. Outlier Construction’s goal is always to complete quality projects at a fair cost while obtaining the best possible products for the owner.

Expert Team

Our company employs some of the most vetted commercial building professionals in Southern Oregon. Our team has extensive experience completing diversified construction projects of all types. Our crew’s specialties include excavation, concrete, rough carpentry, finish work, and erection of pre-engineered metal buildings.
