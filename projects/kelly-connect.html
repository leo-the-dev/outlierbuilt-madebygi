Kelly Connect

The Kelley Connect Building is a two-story, 20,296 square foot multi-use office building. The structure consists of structural steel and wood stud framing, highlighted by a unique architectural entrance, leading into the main lobby. The first floor is a custom-modern layout specifically designed for the owners of a IT support company, the second story is an open floor plan allowing for multiple tenant configurations.
